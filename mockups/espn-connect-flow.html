WHY IT WORKS THIS WAY

•
Yahoo gives apps a "Sign in" button. ESPN doesn't — so the user hands their league over from their own logged-in browser instead.
•
That handoff gives FBL a guest pass it can keep using, so the server refreshes the league on its own — no passwords, ever.
•
The one trade-off: the setup needs a computer once. Phones can't do the handoff — but after it's done once, the phone works forever.
