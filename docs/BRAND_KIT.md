# League Blitz — Brand and Product Brief

Hand this to anyone setting up social accounts, writing marketing copy, or
creating content for League Blitz.

---

## What League Blitz Is

League Blitz is a free fantasy football dashboard that connects all of your
leagues (Yahoo, Sleeper, and ESPN) into one place. Instead of jumping between
three different apps to check scores, set lineups, and manage trades, you see
everything on one screen.

It is a web app (not a native mobile app), works on any device, and can be
installed to your phone's home screen for an app-like experience.

## Key Features (in plain language)

**Multi-Platform Dashboard**
Connect Yahoo, Sleeper, and ESPN leagues and see all your matchups, standings,
and scores in one place. No more switching between three apps.

**Game Day View**
On game day, see every one of your matchups across every league on one screen
with live scoring during NFL windows.

**AI Coach**
An AI-powered "Coach" that analyzes your matchups, evaluates trades, gives
start/sit advice, and writes weekly recaps. It uses your actual league data
(real scores, injuries, bye weeks, weather) rather than generic rankings. When
a call is too close, it says so honestly instead of faking confidence.

**Start/Sit Advisor**
Pick two players on your roster, get Coach's call with a calibrated lean
(strong, moderate, or coin flip). Grounded in your league's actual scoring,
not name recognition.

**AI Trade Analyzer**
Thinking about a trade? Get an analysis grounded in both rosters, standings,
recent form, injuries, and bye weeks. Sharable verdict cards.

**Power Rankings**
PPG-based rankings with trend arrows and weekly awards across all your leagues.

**Trophy Case**
Your real fantasy history: past championships, runner-ups, and records pulled
from your actual league data.

**Weekly Recap**
After each week, see your record across all leagues with an AI-written
narrative recap of the biggest moments.

**Odds Tab**
NFL game lines and player prop data. See which of YOUR players have notable
props each week.

**Draft Kit**
Free snake draft strategy guide, auction draft guide, and a printable cheat
sheet. Available at leagueblitz.app/draft.

**Push Notifications**
Game-day alerts: touchdowns, close games, finals, and lineup warnings.
Game events only, never promotions.

**Cross-Platform Waiver Intel**
See who's trending on Sleeper and available in your leagues (Yahoo, Sleeper,
ESPN).

## Who It's For

Fantasy football managers who play in multiple leagues across different
platforms. The typical user has 2 to 5 leagues spread across Yahoo, Sleeper,
and ESPN and is tired of checking three separate apps every Sunday.

Demographics: primarily 21 to 45, US-based, male-skewing (like fantasy
football in general). Ranges from casual players in one work league to
hardcore managers running 5+ dynasty and redraft leagues.

## Brand Voice and Tone

- **Confident but not cocky.** We know the product is good. We don't need to
  oversell it.
- **Casual and direct.** Talk like you're in a fantasy group chat, not writing
  a press release. Short sentences. No corporate speak.
- **Knowledgeable.** We understand fantasy football deeply. References to real
  strategy (tiers over rankings, position runs, waiver timing) land well with
  this audience.
- **Honest.** The AI Coach says "coin flip" when a call is close. The app
  doesn't promise to make you win your league. It promises to save you time
  and give you better information.
- **No emojis in the product itself.** Social posts can use them sparingly,
  but the app and brand identity are clean and icon-driven, not emoji-heavy.

## Visual Identity

- **Name:** League Blitz
- **Monogram:** LB
- **Primary color:** Amber/gold accent on a dark background

  broadcast feel. Think ESPN or NFL Network graphics, not playful or cartoony.
- **Fonts:** Bebas Neue (display/headlines), Rajdhani (body/UI text)
- **Icons:** Lucide icon set (line icons, no fills)
- **Logo:** Available at leagueblitz.app/icon-512.png (square app icon) and
  the SVG logo renders on the site header

## What NOT to Say

- Don't call it a "betting app" or imply it helps people gamble. It shows odds
  as informational content for fantasy context.
- Don't promise it will help people "win their league." It gives better
  information and saves time.
- Don't use "free trial" language. It is free, period. No trial, no credit
  card, no catch.
- Don't reference the old name "Family Business League" or "Family Biz
  Football." That was the founder's personal league name and is no longer
  associated with the product.
